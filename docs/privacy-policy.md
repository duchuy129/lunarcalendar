# Privacy Policy for Lunar Calendar

**Last Updated:** January 4, 2026

---

## Our Commitment to Privacy

Lunar Calendar ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices.

---

## Data Collection

**We collect ZERO personal data.**

The App:
- ❌ Does NOT collect any personal information
- ❌ Does NOT track your usage
- ❌ Does NOT use analytics services
- ❌ Does NOT use advertising networks
- ❌ Does NOT require an account or login
- ❌ Does NOT connect to the internet (except for app updates)

---

## How the App Works

All lunar calendar calculations are performed **locally on your device** using built-in calendar systems. No data is transmitted to any server.

---

## Data Storage

The only data stored on your device:
- **App settings** (language preference, display options)
- **Calendar cache** (for faster performance)

This data:
- Stays on your device only
- Is never transmitted anywhere
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app
- Is not accessible by any other apps

---

## Security

Since we don't collect or transmit any data, there is no data to secure or protect. All calendar calculations happen locally on your device.

---

- Analytics providers

- Social media integrations
