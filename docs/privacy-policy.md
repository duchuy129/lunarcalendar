**By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.**

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

When you create an account with our App, we collect:

- **Email Address**: Used for account creation, authentication, and communication
- **Password**: Encrypted and stored securely for account access
- **Display Name** (optional): For personalization within the App

In future updates, the App may allow you to:

- Device type (iPhone, iPad, Android)
- Operating system version
- App version
- Device language settings

#### Usage Data
- Features accessed within the App
- Crash reports and error logs (if errors occur)

#### Network Information
- API endpoint response times
- Sync status and timestamps

### 4. Information We DO NOT Collect

We do **NOT** collect:
- ❌ Location data or GPS coordinates
- ❌ Contacts or address book
- ❌ Photos or media files
- ❌ Microphone or camera access
- ❌ SMS or phone call data
- ❌ Payment information (the App is free)
- ❌ Browsing history from other apps
- ❌ Social media profiles or connections

## Data Storage and Security

### Where We Store Your Data

- **API Server**: Hosted on DigitalOcean (cloud infrastructure)
  - Location: United States (New York data center)
  - Security: Industry-standard encryption and access controls

- **Local Device Storage**:
  - SQLite database on your device for offline access
  - Encrypted using iOS/Android native encryption
  - Data is isolated and only accessible by the App

### Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

1. **Authentication**
   - JWT (JSON Web Token) authentication
   - Bcrypt password hashing (industry standard)
   - Passwords are NEVER stored in plain text

2. **Data Transmission**
   - HTTPS/TLS encryption for all API communications
   - Certificate pinning (future enhancement)

3. **Server Security**
   - Firewall protection
   - Regular security updates
   - Access logging and monitoring
   - Security headers (X-Frame-Options, CSP, etc.)

4. **Data Minimization**
   - We only collect data necessary for app functionality
   - No tracking pixels or third-party analytics (MVP)

---

## Data Retention

### How Long We Keep Your Data

- **Account Data**: Retained while your account is active
- **Offline Cache**: Stored on device until you clear cache or uninstall app
- **Logs**: Kept for 90 days for debugging, then automatically deleted

### Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time:
1. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account
2. Confirm deletion
3. All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

**Note**: Cached data on your device will be removed immediately upon account deletion or app uninstallation.

## Your Rights

### Under GDPR (EU Users)

You have the right to:
1. **Access**: Request a copy of your data
2. **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
3. **Erasure**: Delete your account and data ("right to be forgotten")
4. **Portability**: Export your data in a machine-readable format
5. **Restriction**: Limit how we process your data
6. **Object**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
7. **Withdraw Consent**: Stop processing based on consent

### Under CCPA (California Users)

California residents have the right to:
1. Know what personal information is collected
2. Know if data is sold or disclosed (we do NOT sell data)
3. Delete personal information
4. Opt-out of data sales (not applicable - we don't sell data)
5. Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

### How to Exercise Your Rights

Contact us at: **privacy@lunarcalendar.app** (or your designated email)

We will respond within:
- **30 days** for GDPR requests
- **45 days** for CCPA requests
